About Us

At Removal and Demolition, we specialize in safe, reliable, and professional asbestos demolition and removal services. With a deep understanding of the risks associated with asbestos, our trained and certified team follows strict safety standards to protect your health, your property, and the environment. We are committed to providing transparent communication, efficient project management, and high-quality service from start to finish. Whether it’s a residential, commercial, or industrial site, we handle every job with the utmost care and responsibility. Our mission is to make asbestos removal worry-free for you, ensuring complete compliance with local laws and environmental regulations. At Removal and Demolition, there are no shortcuts and no surprises — just honest work, safe practices, and a dedication to excellence you can trust. Your safety is our top priority, and we are proud to be your trusted partner for asbestos demolition and removal needs.

KMD Demolition & Asbestos Removal has a team dedicated to the safe removal and replacement of roofs containing asbestos. We use environmentally safe disposal methods for hazardous materials.

Our story

Removal and Demolition began with a simple goal — to protect people by eliminating hazardous materials from their homes and workplaces. What started as a local effort quickly grew into a trusted service across the region.

We saw a need. Too many families and workers were unknowingly exposed to the harmful effects of asbestos. With that in mind, we built our company on the foundation of honesty, safety, and professionalism.

Over the years, we've expanded our services and sharpened our skills. Every project we complete is done with precision, care, and a deep respect for our clients' safety. Whether it's a small residential job or a large commercial demolition, we treat each one with the same level of dedication.

Our story is still unfolding — and we’re proud to be making spaces cleaner, safer, and healthier every step of the way.
